CICLING 2014

Dr. Basanta Joshi, Research Director @ LogpPoint and me during CICLING 2014
me speaking @ CICLING 2014 on our clustering algorithm for log messages streams.
me with our poster for CICLING 2014.

Research Team from LogPoint presented a conference paper at CICLING 2014 on Clustering algorithm for streams of log messages.

Read about the paper here.

Our algorithm works in two steps. First, it sketeches the log messages using Bloom filter, then uses modified version of Sequential Leader Clustering to cluster messages based on sketched representation. The notion of similarity used was Jaccard similarity for bit-strings.
