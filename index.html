I'm Umanga Bista , originally from Kathmandu and currently a PhD candidate at the Computational media lab, Australian National University .

My PhD supevisors are Dr. Lexing Xie , Dr. Aditya Menon and Dr. Lizhen Qu.

I am interested in knowledge graphs and deep learning research. I also like to work with data setreaming systems and fascinated by functional programming.
